There are three main species of coffee beans.

  1. Arabica - Most favored
  2. Robusta - Cheapest
  3. Liberica - Not so known

Arabica is seen as a higher quality bean due to its taste. However it can be difficult to grow, as it requires very specific growing conditions and is also suseptable to various diseases and pests.

Robusta is seen as a harder plant that grows larger than Arabica, and also has a higher amount of caffiene content.

Liberica has a caffiene content in between Arabica and Robusta, mostly famous in the Phillipines and is grown in South Asia.

Varierty Caffiene Content
Arabica 0.6% - 1.2%
Robusta 1.8% - 3%
Liberica 1.2% - 1.5%
